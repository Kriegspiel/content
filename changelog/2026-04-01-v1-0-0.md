Version `1.0.0` marks the first full playable public release.

Highlights:
- Player accounts, login, registration, lobby flow, and game creation are live in the production app.
- Human-vs-human and human-vs-bot games are playable end to end on `app.kriegspiel.org`.
- Player-specific referee logs and Kriegspiel scoresheets are persisted in backend game state and shown privately to the correct side only.
- The game UI was stabilized across desktop and mobile, including clocks, status layout, hover behavior, phantom interactions, and no-jump move handling.
- Ruleset selection is part of game creation, and the chosen ruleset is visible during play.
- A first model-driven bot joined the platform alongside the random bot.

In short: by `1.0.0`, Kriegspiel was no longer just rules and contracts. It was a working online game.
